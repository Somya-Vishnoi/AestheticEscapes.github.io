Hidden Gems of India

Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Explore the barren beauty of this Himalayan desert with its ancient monasteries and scenic trails.

Best Time to Visit: May to September

Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

Known for its lush green landscapes, vibrant festivals, and Apatani tribal culture.

Best Time to Visit: March to October

Gokarna, Karnataka

A serene alternative to Goa, Gokarna offers pristine beaches and spiritual charm.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Mawlynnong, Meghalaya

Dubbed the cleanest village in Asia, Mawlynnong is a nature lover's paradise with living root bridges.

Best Time to Visit: June to September

Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Hampi is a treasure trove of ancient ruins and breathtaking landscapes.

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Chettinad, Tamil Nadu

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Known for its unique cuisine and palatial mansions, Chettinad is a journey into Tamil heritage.

Lonar Lake, Maharashtra

Best Time to Visit: November to February

A fascinating saltwater lake formed by a meteor impact, surrounded by temples and lush greenery.

Mandarmani, West Bengal

Best Time to Visit: November to February

A tranquil beach destination with golden sands, perfect for a peaceful getaway in Eastern India.

Mystical Mornings in Banaras

There’s something otherworldly about watching the sunrise over the ghats of Varanasi. As dawn breaks, the Ganges glows in hues of orange and gold, reflecting the vibrant life that awakens along its banks. Priests in saffron robes chant morning prayers, the air thick with incense and the quiet hum of history. It’s as if time has stood still in this sacred city. Walk the ghats, join the boat rides at dawn, and let the city’s mysticism work its way into your soul.

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Tour Dates: November 5, December 15, January 20


Agra - The City of Eternal Love and Legacy

Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, is a city that resonates with romance and history. Located on the banks of the Yamuna River, Agra's architectural marvels from the Mughal era stand as timeless testaments to India’s rich heritage. Beyond the Taj Mahal, the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri offer glimpses into the grandeur of a bygone era. When in Agra, don’t miss trying the delectable petha, a sweet treat that’s synonymous with the city. The best time to visit Agra is from October to March when the weather is pleasant for exploring its monuments. A sunrise view of the Taj Mahal will leave you enchanted for a lifetime.

Best Time to visit: October to March.

Tour Dates: February 10-12 (Spring Delights)
November 18-20 (Mughal Heritage Walk)


Darjeeling - The Queen of Hills

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Darjeeling is a paradise for nature lovers and tea connoisseurs. Known for its lush tea gardens and the breathtaking view of Mount Kanchenjunga, this charming hill station exudes tranquility. A ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, fondly called the "Toy Train," takes you on a nostalgic journey through scenic landscapes.

Best Time to Visit: March to May, September to November

Tour Dates: April 5-7 (Spring Blossoms)
October 12-14 (Autumn Escapes)


Manali - Adventures in the Himalayas

Manali, nestled in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, is a haven for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Whether it's trekking through lush trails, paragliding over breathtaking valleys, or simply soaking in the beauty of the Beas River, Manali offers an unmatched blend of adventure and serenity.

Best Time to Visit: October to February (snow), May to June (trekking)

Tour Dates: January 10-12 (Winter Wonderland)
June 15-17 (Summer Adventure Trek)
